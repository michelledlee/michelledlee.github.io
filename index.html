Hi! I'm Michelle, a Canadian national who made her way to Silicon Valley to make her break into tech a reality. My journey onto the tech scene was nonconventional. Many years ago, I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration. I went on to complete a CPA and worked in various accounting roles. I always felt like I was more interested in working on projects that would directly impact customers or improve operations within the company. I started coding on my own and fell in love with it. I decided to formalize my computer science education. I was admitted to the Align program at Northeastern which is meant to bridge the gap for those that don't have a background in computer science. I successfully finished the bridging program and completed a Master's in Computer Science. During my time in school, I got a real taste of what it was like working in the industry when I worked as a Web Development Intern at Seagate Technology. I'm excited to embark on the next step in my journey. I'm searching for a career opportunity where I can continue learning and growing.
